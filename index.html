About us

Owa Academy

Mfundo Owami Ndaba founded Owa Academy in July 2020, we provide lessons for kids from 8-16 years. They learn different skills that are focused on empowering young kids with skills that are essential for the 4th Industrial Revolution. This passion for learning these skills was brought because of the love technology and working with our hands. We believe that practice makes perfect. The more you do something you get better at it. Kids start having digital gadgets from as young as 8 years.

It is started that kids have minds that are like a sponge meaning that they learn quicker. We want to take advantage of this crucial time in their development to help them gain skills that can provide an income for the.
There is a high unemployment rate in our beautiful country which is a huge problem. The skills that the kids gain help them to be problem solvers and provide innovative solutions.


We live in a world were technology is replacing jobs. The president spoke about Robotics and Smart Cities in the State of the Nation Address. Our service offering are aligned to skills sets needed for the future. Give your child the head start by joining Owa Robotics.
